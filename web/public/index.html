Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Smart Call Decline.

Will this replace my existing booking app?

No, it works alongside your current tools. Smart Call Decline captures leads and syncs with your existing calendar and booking system. It's designed to fill the gap when you can't answer calls on-site.

Does it work on any smartphone?

Yes, it works with any smartphone and carrier. No special app required. We integrate with your existing phone service to handle call routing and SMS responses automatically.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption and never store sensitive customer information. All lead data is processed securely and can be deleted at any time. We're fully GDPR compliant.

What if I need to take an emergency call?

You can easily toggle the system on/off or set up emergency bypass numbers (like family or existing customers). The system is designed to be flexible around your real needs.

How quickly can I get started?

Setup takes about 60 seconds. You'll answer a few questions about your business, we'll configure your SMS responses, and you're ready to capture leads immediately.
